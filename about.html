From Health Care to Coding

My name is Colin Peterson.

I am a computer programming student located in Peterborough, Ontario. I am also a Registered Practical Nurse, and have my AEMCA certification as a Primary Care Paramedic in Ontario.

After working in health care for years I decided it was time to make a change. Programming and technology have been hobbies and passions of mine for many years. I decided to turn this passion into a career.

I have experience in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, C# and SQL.

I am currently working towards a diploma in computer programming from Confederation College in Thunder Bay, Ontario.
